ASP.NET AJAX Controls

Accelerate your application development with ASP.NET AJAX controls built on the Aikido™ Framework to be the fastest, lightest, and most complete toolset for rapidly building high performance web applications.

The Most Agile Toolset for Your Web Apps, Anywhere

Build intuitive, full-featured business applications for any web browser with NetAdvantage for ASP.NET - the most powerful set of high performance UX controls and components available anywhere. And boasting the world’s fastest Data Grid, the performance of our controls is unrivaled.

Write Once, Run Everywhere

Build Web and iOS mobile applications with the versatility and performance users expect to find only in desktop applications. All of our ASP.NET controls have been tested for cross-browser compatibility - including iPad and iPhone browsers. Our controls are also Touch-friendly and support a range of gestures - like flicking, pinching, panning and zooming – making it easy to interact with your data on the go.

   
  

Complete Controls

You get not one but two ASP.NET AJAX grids (Data Grid and Hierarchical Data Grid), charts, calendars, menus, trees, PDF and XPS exporters, MS Excel and MS Word libraries, tabs, rich WYSIWYG HTML editing, CAPTCHA, explorer bars, data entry editors, the HTML5 video player and ASP.NET upload control… You get the point. We’ve been adding and enhancing our toolset for ASP.NET since 2002. No other toolset is more complete or offers the breadth and depth of feature functionality as NetAdvantage. Just configure and go!

Killer Performance, Killer Apps

If you have massive amounts of data that require lightning-fast processing with pinpoint accuracy, only the industry’s top-performing controls will do. No other Data Grid on the market even comes close to the Data Grid you get with NetAdvantage for ASP.NET. The proof is in the pudding – check out the whitepaper.

  
  

Solid Architecture for Speedy App Development

Time is money. Accelerate your development with ASP.NET AJAX controls, built upon the Infragistics Aikido Framework to be the lightest and fastest toolset around. The Aikido Framework offers modularity, built-in inheritance scenarios, intuitive API built-in AJAX support, Web 2.0 rendering (CSS, HTML), accessibility support and more. And with backward compatibility, we help you go beyond applications, to building great user experiences in any web browser.

Maximum Support for Microsoft Platforms

Get support for the latest Microsoft platforms. ASP.NET is fully compatible with Visual Studio 2012 - including design time, Toolbox and dialogs - and every control is tested to support Internet Explorer 10.

  
  

Resources Beyond What You'll Find in the Box

Get a rapid response and immediate support when you need it, 24 hours a day/5 days a week – via phone, email, or by chat. When you need to brainstorm, join our community of over 850,000 developers from the most popular software companies to the most data-demanding enterprises. You can also tap into our sample code, videos, and documentation to keep your project on - or ahead of - target.
